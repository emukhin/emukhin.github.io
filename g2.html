Want to book your Ontario G2 road test to an earlier date?

Many road test centres are fully booked for months in advance. If you need your license sooner, your only hope is to snap up a cancellation when it becomes available. But these openings don't last long, and you can't spend all of your time refreshing the DriveTest.ca appointment finder.

Our unique software monitors cancellations in real-time, 24/7. We're constantly searching for openings, day and night, and we book your driver's test appointment the moment a slot that fits your request becomes available.

When you book through our site, you only pay our variable booking fee if we find a booking that falls within your requested dates, at your preferred location.

Save time and money by using our booking service.
