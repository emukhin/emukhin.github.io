What Is the G1 Test & Why You Need It

To begin the process of becoming a licensed driver in Ontario, you must pass the G1 knowledge test. The official test is offered by the Ministry of Transportation at DriveTest Centres across the province.

After passing the written G1 exam, you have one year to study for the G2 road test. To avoid hassle and delay, you can book your G2 driving test directly through our site.

Our road test booking site is perfect for learners, because by using our service, you're more likely to get the test date you want exactly when you're ready to take it. Keep that in mind as you prep for your G1 exam!

How Our G1 Prep Quiz Works

When you take our G1 road test prep quiz, you'll answer 40 random multiple choice questions. There are 20 questions on road rules and 20 questions on road signs.

The required score to pass the MTO G1 test is 80%, and it's the same for our mock exam. To pass, you must answer 32 questions correctly: 16 out of 20 in each section, road rules and signs respectively.

Every time you take the test, you'll face a new set of random questions.

You can take our G1 prep quiz as many times as you like. In fact, we encourage you to repeat the quiz until you can get all the answers correct, each time.
