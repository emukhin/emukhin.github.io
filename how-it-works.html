About:

Taking a road test is pretty stressful by itself and we are trying to ease this by at least taking the stress out of finding the nearest cancellation date for your road test. We are not affiliated with either Ministry of Transportation or the DriveTest, we are just offering the best fully automated 24/7 service of finding the nearest cancellation date for your road test in Ontario.

Before you book your road test appointment with us, please review these important requirements.

  1. All road test fees must be prepaid at the time of booking an appointment with us.
  2. Make sure you can access you DriveTest dashboard by visiting drivetest.ca web site

That's basically it and now you are ready to book your road test with us by filling in the form with your data* and the time that best suits you. Location picker supports multiple selections. You are able to create complex conditions by adding additional periods. All times are in the Eastern Time Zone.

*Your personal data such as Driver's Licence Number, Driver's Licence Expiry, Driver's email will not be used for any other purposes besides rescheduling your road test.

What happens next?

Once you submit your request it is started processing instantly. All requests are processed on first come first serve basis which means that if somebody requested the same location and time period even a minute before you, you'll be second in line.

Once your order is fulfilled we'll notify you by email. We'll also send you an invoice for a service fee of $40 via paypal. Otherwise, if we were unable to fulfil your order you'll be notified of it free of charge.

If you change your mind

Should your plans have changed, just email us as soon as possible and we'll cancel your order chargeless. Please consider the nature of how the service works. Since it's fully automatic it may be impossible to cancel the order at the time you requested. In the worst scenario your appointment will be rescheduled but we won't charge you in this case.
